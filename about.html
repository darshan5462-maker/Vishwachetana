Message from Chairperson

Dr. Vijayalakshmi Veeramachaneni

Chairperson, CHETANA Group of Institutions — Davanagere

"Chetana Group of Institutions has a proud legacy of 38 years of academic excellence, proudly serving the centre of Karnataka region. Our institutions have established themselves as a benchmark for quality education, shaping the minds of future generations."

Over the years, we have consistently delivered outstanding results, with our students excelling in various fields, including academics, sports, and extracurricular activities.

  • Exceptional Academic Results: Students consistently achieve outstanding results in JEE, NEET, NATA & KCET.
  • Innovative Programs: Cutting-edge STEAM, arts, and humanities programs for unique learning opportunities.
  • Community Service: Students engaged in social responsibility projects across Davanagere.
  • Award-Winning Institution: "Inspiring Woman Award 2021" and numerous state-level honours.
